Book·Modern, 2021·~22 hr read

The Dawn of Everything

David Graeber & David Wengrow

The standard story goes hunter-gatherer, then farmer, then state, in that order. Graeber and Wengrow argue early societies actually experimented with far more political arrangements than that, and some of them worked.
